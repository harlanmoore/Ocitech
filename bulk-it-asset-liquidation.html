Built for recurring volume, not one-off sales

If your organization regularly generates surplus memory, GPUs, or server hardware — as an ITAD company, recycler, or enterprise IT department — we're set up to work as a standing buyer rather than a one-time transaction. Consistent, direct pricing on recurring lots saves both sides the overhead of requoting from scratch every time.

Why recyclers and ITADs work with us

Many recyclers end up shredding or scrapping memory and GPUs that are actually worth real money in the current market, simply because sorting and testing takes time they don't have budgeted. We take that inventory as-is — mixed, untested, partial — and handle the grading ourselves.
